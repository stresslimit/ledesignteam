Who

Founded in 2013 in New York, Le Design Team is a Design Creative Organization that brings projects to life by way of user experience and organizational design, product strategy and technical consulting.

Partners

Céline Semaan is a visionary user experience, visual and organizational designer, working for companies from retail, ecommerce, content, government and educational sectors. She has also taught UX design and strategy in Montreal and New York, notably designing curriculum and teaching the core UX intensive courses at General Assembly. She is an MIT Media Lab Director‘s Fellow, serves on the Board of Directors of AIGA NY in charge of events and programming bridging the gap between design, social change and human rights. Her fashion activism label Slow Factory produces meaningful accessories which have been worn by Alicia Keys and Pharrell Williams and is sold at the MoMA store NYC.

Colin Vernon is a veteran entrepreneur and seasoned leader of digital products, across Product, Design and Engineering. Keeping a deep techincal knowledge while growing and leading teams, and driving product and company strategy, he ran Stresslimit Design, a successful Montreal digital agency for a decade; helped found, ran all software and built a connected IoT Platform for littleBits Electronics, and has helped many companies with core ditigal and product strategy, leadership and technical implementation such as Beyoncé Digital and Medium.com.
